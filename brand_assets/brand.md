# Skyline Greens — Brand Reference

> Urban agriculture with architectural precision.
> **Brand pillars:** Premium · Modern · Engineered · Calm · Trustworthy

---

## Logo

| File | Usage |
|------|-------|
| `logo-primary.png` | Default — dark green on light/white backgrounds |
| `logo-mist.png` | Light mint variant — use on dark backgrounds |

**Rules:**
- Always use icon + wordmark as a single unit. Never separate them.
- Minimum print size: 32mm wide. Digital: 180px wide.
- No shadows, outlines, bevels, or effects.
- Do not stretch, skew, or re-draw the icon.
- Clearspace = height of the 'I' in SKYLINE on all sides.

## Typography

**Primary typeface: Manrope**

| Role | Weight | Notes |
|------|--------|-------|
| SKYLINE (wordmark) | Bold / SemiBold | Wider tracking — confidence |
| GREENS (wordmark) | Regular / Medium | Matching tracking — calm |
| Headings | Bold | Tight tracking (-0.03em) |
| Body | Regular | Generous line-height (1.7) |

**Objective:** Confidence, clarity, scalability.

## Iconography

All icons use rounded-precision geometry. Use on dark backgrounds with a white fill.

| File | Label |

**Icon rules:**
- Flat fills, crisp edges only.
- Thin architectural rules at 10–20% opacity.
- No shadows, bevels, or heavy gradients.
- Never stretch, skew, or re-draw.

## Graphic Language

- Use generous whitespace — the luxury of space signals premium positioning.
- Rounded-precision geometry echoing the icon's panel radii.
- Flat fills, crisp edges, thin architectural rules at 10–20% opacity.
- Do NOT place the full logo over busy or noisy photography.
